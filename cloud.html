FAQ

  1. How long do Rocket.Chat Cloud trials last?

    Every plan has a 30 day free trial. You can choose to cancel your plan anytime during the trial.

  2. When will I be billed after my trial ends?

    We will send you an email reminder when your trial is close to ending. When your trial ends, you will charged for your current plan if you have not closed your account and correct billing information has been added to your account.

  3. What is the billing period?

    All plans are billed monthly.

  4. When can I cancel my plan?

    You can cancel your plan at any time and continue to use your plan for the remainder of your current paid-for month.

  5. How do I report an issue and get support for Rocket.Chat Cloud?

    Email cloud.support@rocket.chat

  6. What is the Rocket.Chat Cloud uptime SLA?

    We guarantee 99.9% monthly uptime. If we fall short of our 99.9% guarantee, Medium and Large plan customers can contacts us to get account credit to the value of: 100 x verified down time / plan monthly cost.

  7. Do you offer discounts for NGOs and educational institutes?

    Yes if you run or work for a non-profit or educational institute you may be eligible to receive discounted rates on Rocket.Chat Cloud Hosting.
